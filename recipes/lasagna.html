Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, cheese, and rich meat sauce.

It is hearty, comforting, and perfect for family dinners.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Onion & garlic

Steps

  1. Cook the noodles.
  2. Prepare the meat sauce.
  3. Layer noodles, sauce, and cheese.
  4. Bake in oven at 180°C for 30 minutes.
