World's Best Lasagna

Description

This lasagna recipe is the ultimate comfort food. Layered with a rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and melted mozzarella, it's a crowd-pleaser that tastes even better the next day.

Making it from scratch takes a bit of time, but the depth of flavor from the slow-simmered sauce is well worth the effort.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • 3/4 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans tomato paste

Steps

  1. Meat sauce: Cook sausage, beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until browned.
  2. Simmer: Stir in tomatoes and paste. Season with parsley, basil, and salt. Simmer for about 1.5 hours.
  3. Assemble: Layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella in a baking dish.
  4. Bake: Cover with foil and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 25 minutes.
