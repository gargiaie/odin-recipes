Homemade Vegetable Momos Recipe

A popular Indian street-style snack, these steamed dumplings are filled with finely chopped, crunchy vegetables.

Ingredients

For the Dough

  • 2 cups All-purpose flour (Maida) [6]
  • ½ teaspoon Salt [3]
  • 1 teaspoon Oil [6]
  • Water for kneading (approx. ½ to ¾ cup) [6]

For the Filling

  • 1 tablespoon Oil [2]
  • 1 tablespoon Garlic, finely minced [6]
  • 1 tablespoon Ginger, finely minced [6]
  • 1-2 Green Chilies, finely chopped [6]
  • 2 cups Cabbage, finely shredded/grated [6]
  • 1 cup Carrot, grated [6]
  • 1 medium Onion, finely chopped [6]
  • ¼ cup Spring Onions, chopped [6]
  • 1 tablespoon Soy Sauce [6]
  • ½ teaspoon Black Pepper Powder [6]
  • Salt to taste [6]

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Dough: In a bowl, mix flour, salt, and oil. Add water gradually to knead a firm yet soft dough. Cover with a moist napkin and rest for 30 minutes [1, 3].
  2. Prepare the Filling: Heat oil in a pan, add garlic, ginger, and green chilies. Sauté for 10 seconds. Add onion and stir-fry for 1 minute [3].
  3. Add cabbage, carrot, and spring onions. Stir-fry on high heat for 3-4 minutes until the veggies are cooked but still crunchy [3].
  4. Add soy sauce, black pepper, and salt. Mix well and let the filling cool completely [6].
  5. Shape the Momos: Knead the dough again, divide into small balls. Roll each ball into a thin circle (approx. 3-4 inch diameter), keeping edges thinner than the center [3].
  6. Place 1-2 tablespoons of filling in the center. Pleat the edges, gathering everything to seal in the middle [3].
  7. Steam: Grease the steamer with oil. Place momos and steam for 8-10 minutes over high heat until the outer covering looks translucent [3].
  8. Serve hot with spicy garlic chutney!
