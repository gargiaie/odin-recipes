Homemade Margherita Pizza

Prep time: 2 hrs | Bake time: 10-15 mins

Ingredients

  • 1 lb pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 8 oz mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Fresh basil leaves

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 500°F (260°C) with a pizza stone inside.
  2. Roll out the dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread pizza sauce, leaving 1 inch for the crust.
  4. Add mozzarella cheese and toppings.
  5. Bake for 10-15 minutes until crust is golden.
